Fujifilm X-T4 Camera

Category: Cameras

Details:

The Fujifilm XT4 is a high-performance mirrorless camera that is perfect for photographers of all levels. With its 26.1 MP APS-C X-Trans CMOS 4 Sensor, 5-axis In-body Image Stabilization and 4K Video Recording capabilities, it's sure to take your photography to the next level. The 3.69M-dot OLED Electronic Viewfinder and 3.0" 1.04m-dot Tilting Touchscreen LCD allows you to easily compose and review your shots.

  • 5-axis In-body Image Stabilization
  • 4K Video Recording
